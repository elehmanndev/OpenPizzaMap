Pizza Lovers Say

Pepe in Grani is widely praised for Franco Pepe's obsessive dough work — long fermentation, light hand with toppings, and a willingness to put a margherita sfizio alongside more experimental constructions. Reviewers consistently mention the value of the multi-course tasting menus and the calm of the Caiazzo village setting, an hour outside Naples.
